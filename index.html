What we use

DJI Phantom 4

Owned
since 2016

The Phantom 4 Pro was one of the first drone to be part of this company. This incredible machine is packed with amazing features, such as auto target detection, 4k at 60FPS and 30 minutes flight time. With this amazing piece of tech, you are guaranteed to receive a refined product.

DJI Inspire 2

Owned since 2018

Our most recent investment toward Drone Filmer is the DJI Inspire 2. Paired with an ZENMUSE X7 lens, this drone is very difficult to compete against. Used by many professionals in the world, the Inspire 2 can film in 6k, and the low latency makes the drone perfect for live streaming an event.

DJI Mavic Pro

Owned since 2018

The Mavic Pro is a small yet powerful drone. It's portability makes it possible for us to expand our services to more remote or hard to access areas. At 4k 30FPS, this drone still delivers amazing video, with a wide colour range and fast processing.

DJI Ronin M

Owned since 2017

As a filming company, we also partake in traditional filming, having experience with RED cameras and DSLRs. For optimal stabilisation and smooth playback, the DJI Ronin M is one of the best DSLR and Video camera stabilisers we have had the chance to use.
